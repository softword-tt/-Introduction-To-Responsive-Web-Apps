Multiple-column Layout

Multiple-column Layout is closely related to Paged Media, in that each column box becomes a fragment, much like a printed page becomes a fragment of an overall document. Therefore the properties now defined in the CSS Fragmentationspecification are required in order to control how content breaks between columns.
